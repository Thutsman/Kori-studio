Our Story

A sanctuary for
slow living & creativity

Nestled in the leafy suburb of Ilanda, Kori Studio is more than a cafe — it is a breathing space. Named after the graceful Kori bustard, our sanctuary invites you to pause, savour, and create.

Under canvas canopies and among garden palms, we serve thoughtfully crafted food in an atmosphere of calm warmth. Every plate is a canvas; every visit, an invitation to reconnect with yourself and your community.

Whether you come for morning coffee, a long lunch with friends, or an evening of art and conversation, Kori Studio holds space for you — safe, serene, and beautifully alive.

Art & Creativity

Where plates become
canvases

Kori Studio is a living gallery. From palette-shaped condiment dishes to rotating local artwork on our walls, creativity flows through every corner of our space.

Join us for open-mic evenings, art workshops, live acoustic sessions, and community gatherings. We believe nourishment extends beyond the table — it lives in expression, connection, and the quiet joy of making something beautiful.
